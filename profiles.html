Squad Numbers

Bar Hill Cricket Club members may have their own squad number between 1 and 99. Any new members (and any existing members that have not got a squad number) can request one. Please check the squad number list for available numbers. Numbers will be allocated on a first come, first served basis. Once a squad number has been allocated it will stay with that player until 12 months after they cease membership of the club.

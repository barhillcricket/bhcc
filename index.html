Youth Training
Youth training nights will start on Wednesday 24th April at the village green, from 6-7pm (weather permitting), with coaches Dave Allsopp and Peter Stockton once again in charge. New Players Required
Bar Hill Cricket Club currently has an adult Saturday league team competing in the CCA Junior League and a Midweek team competing in the CBHL The club also arranges several friendly matches each season. We always welcome new players of any ability. For more information or to join, please email: info@barhillcricket.org.uk

Match Reports

Saturday 18th May 2019 - Bar Hill v Steeple Morden - Scorecard
We finally got started in the league with our match against Steeple Morden. Bar Hill won the toss and decided to field on a mildly damp afternoon. The opposition started well, but the strength of the Bar Hill bowling showed, reducing Steeple Morden from 26-0 to 48 all out. The pick of the bowling was with Barry Driver (4-3), Sam Wilson (3-9) and Tom Page (2-19). Jake Curry and Peter Howard started the run chase strongly with 13 and 8 respectively but the regular loss of wickets led to a nervous affair. However Paul Stimpson and Martin Haslop got us over the line in 16.5 overs.

Sunday 29th April 2019 - Girton v Bar Hill - Scorecard
We started our season with a first team friendly at Girton in a match consisting of 30 overs per side. Bar Hill lost the toss and were put in to bat. We posted a strong total of 156-7, with Jim Neal and James Barclay both retiring at 30 Not Out, whilst Jake Curry and Adam Dean chipped in with 26 and 28* respectively. We started well in the field, taking some crucial wickets against their stong batting lineup but dropped catches ended up being our downfall, allowing Girton to score their runs with 10 balls to spare. Barry Driver was the pick of the bowlers with 3-20 from just 4 overs.

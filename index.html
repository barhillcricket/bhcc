Awards Evening
On Saturday 14th September we held our annual awards evening to celebrate the success of the adult teams and the youth teams. The awards are as follows:

Midweek player of the season: Jake Curry
First team batsman: Adam Dean (average 33.67, top score 58, SR 104.12)
First team bowler: Sam Wilson (18 wickets, average 4.61, SR 12.33)
Outstanding performance: Sam Wilson (6-11 V Cambourne)
First team player: Sam Wilson
Most improved player of the year: James Barclay
Duckman: Dave Underhay
Champagne moment:​​ Scott Underhay (Match winning 4 on last ball V Saffron Walden)
Clubman:​​​ Jake Curry
Youth coaches player of the year: Jack Williams
Youth Captains player of the year: Tom Connor
Most improved youth player of the year: Alex Collins
Youth coaches award for good attitude: Theo Goddard
Top youth run scorer: Seb Collins
Top youth wicket taker: Jack Williams

Youth Training
Youth training has now concluded for the year and will resume in the Spring. All ages are welcome and playing equipment is provided. If you would like more information about the youth set up, please contact David Allsopp, Youth Team Manager.

New Players Required
Next year Bar Hill Cricket Club will be looking to run a second adult Saturday league team competing in the CCA Junior League and a Midweek team competing in the CBHL The club also arranges several friendly matches each season. We always welcome new players of any ability. For more information or to join, please email: info@barhillcricket.org.uk

New Concrete Strip
We are very pleased to say we have been awarded £2,000 by the A14 Community Fund towards a concrete base and mat for the net! We plan to have this insalled before the start of next season. We acknowledge the grant we have received from the A14 Community Fund, a fund managed by Cambridgeshire Community Foundation.

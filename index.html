2022 AGM
Our Annual Meeting will take place on Tuesday January 18th at 8pm. We are planning to meet at the Social Club, where there is ventilation and the abillity to be more socially distant than in the meeting room - but we may meet on Zoom depending on the ongoing Covid situation.

As well as financial and other reports, we will also be holding elections for vacant positions on the committee. We are looking for a treasurer and secretary this year. We have split the secretary duties into two roles to make them less of a burden for one person. You will find the role descriptions below.

It is at this meeting that we also elect team captains.

If anyone is interested in any of these posts, please let Paul Stimpson know by message.

If anyone would like to propose any agenda items, please let Paul Stimpson know by next Tuesday (11th). As a reminder, match and membership fees are no longer part of the Annual Meeting agenda.

Role Descriptions:

  • Secretary
  • Minutes Secretary
  • Treasurer - (TBC)

End of Season Presentation
Our end of season presentations will take place at the social club on Saturday September 25th.

All members and their families are welcome from 7pm onwards - the presentations (player of the year, clubman, most improved etc) will be held around 8pm. Food will be provided by the social club.

Please can you let me know by commenting on the Facebook post or emailing Paul Stimpson whether you will be attending and how many family members will be with you. This is to help us work out how much food we need.

Attendance is free but we will be holding a fundraising raffle. If anyone would like to donate a prize, please let me know.

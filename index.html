Latest News

Players Needed
We are looking for new players of all ages for the 2020 season. We are running the following teams:
Under-10 (soft ball)
Under-13
2x Saturday adult teams
1x Midweek adult team
No previous experience is needed and we provide playing equipment. Contact us via info@barhillcricket.org.uk for more information.

Fees
Our fees for the 2020 season are
Adults: £10 annual membership; Saturday match fees of £10 (£5 concessions); Midweek match fees £5 (£3 concessions).
New adult members will not be asked to pay any fees until after they have played two matches (ie before their third match).
Youth players: £25 annual membership for the first child from a family, all subsequent children £15. No match fees.

Youth training
Youth training sessions will be held on the village green every Wednesday from April 22nd (weather permitting) from 6-7pm. Keep an eye on our website and Facebook group for updates. Under-10 home matches will likely be on Mondays and Under-13 home matches on Thursdays.

Quiz Night
Don't miss our fundraising quiz night at the Sports & Social Club on Saturday April 18th (7.30pm for a 7.45pm start). Entry fee is £2 per person for teams of up to 6. Members, family and friends of all ages welcome! The Social Club will have food available to order.

Awards Evening

New Concrete Strip
We are very pleased to say we have been awarded £2,000 by the A14 Community Fund towards a concrete base and mat for the net! We plan to have this insalled before the start of next season. We acknowledge the grant we have received from the A14 Community Fund, a fund managed by Cambridgeshire Community Foundation.

Latest News

AGM
The Bar Hill Cricket Club annual meeting will take place at 8pm on Wednesday January 27th. Due to the pandemic, this will take place on Zoom and we will provide a link for everyone to join the meeting nearer the time.

The main order of business is the election of committee members. This year, we need a new treasurer as Dave Underhay is standing down after a number of years. The committee would like to extend our thanks to Dave for his hard work to help take the club forward. If anyone is interested in this or any other roles in the club, or would like a general chat about how to get involved, please let Paul Stimpson know.

If anyone would like to propose any amendments to the club Constitution please let Paul Stimpson know by Wednesday January 20th. The committee has no amendments to propose.

An agenda will be sent out a week before the meeting and ask for any apologies and proxy votes then.

Coaching Videos
With cricket practice currently suspended we have started creating some videos to help you practice at home. The new coaching page will be updated with videos.

New Concrete Strip
We are very pleased to say we have been awarded £2,000 by the A14 Community Fund towards a concrete base and mat for the net! We plan to have this insalled before the start of next season. We acknowledge the grant we have received from the A14 Community Fund, a fund managed by Cambridgeshire Community Foundation.
